Smart Student Housing Made Simple

Revolutionizing how students in Uganda find safe, convenient, and suitable accommodation near institutions of higher learning.

Bridging the Gap in Student Housing

In Uganda, finding safe, convenient, and suitable student accommodation near institutions of higher learning remains a frustrating and inefficient process. Students—especially those relocating from rural areas or foreign countries—often face significant challenges.

Our solution is a smart, student-centered hostel booking and management mobile application that bridges the gap between students looking for suitable accommodation and hostel owners who want to manage their properties more efficiently.

For Students

Centralized marketplace to discover, compare, and book hostels with detailed filters

For Hostel Owners

Digital management platform for records, payments, vacancies, and communication

Comprehensive Features

Our app provides everything students and hostel owners need for efficient, transparent, and secure accommodation management.

Advanced Search & Filters

Search hostels by distance from university, price range, academic program, disability accommodations, and facilities.

Smart Recommendations

Get suggestions for hostels with students in your program and find compatible roommates by lifestyle and academic focus.

Tenant Records Management

Complete digital records management for hostel owners including bio data, room allocation, and payment history.

Maintenance Tracking

Log complaints and track repairs with real-time notifications from acknowledgment to completion.

Repair Scheduling

Get notified about scheduled maintenance visits with time and date details for better planning.

Management Announcements

Receive important hostel announcements via app notifications, email, or SMS from management.

Online Payments

Secure online booking and rent payments with digital receipts and automated reminders.

Digital Documentation

Electronic lease signing and secure document storage for ID scans and verification documents.
